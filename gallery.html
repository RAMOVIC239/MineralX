Mineral Gallery

Explore some of the world's most valuable minerals and learn about their importance in modern industries and economic development.

Gold

Gold is one of the world's most valuable minerals and is widely used in jewelry, electronics, and financial reserves.

Diamond

Diamonds are valued for their beauty and hardness and are used in jewelry and industrial cutting tools.

Copper

Copper is an excellent conductor of electricity and is essential for electrical wiring and electronics.

Tanzanite

Tanzanite is a rare gemstone found only in Tanzania and is highly valued around the world.

Coal

Coal has long been used as an important source of energy for electricity generation and industrial production.

Iron Ore

Iron ore is the primary raw material used in steel production, making it vital for construction and manufacturing.
