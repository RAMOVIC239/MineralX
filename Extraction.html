Mineral Extraction Methods

Mineral extraction is the process of removing valuable minerals from the earth. The method used depends on the type of mineral, its location, and the surrounding environment.

Surface Mining

Surface mining is used when minerals are located close to the earth's surface. It is one of the most common and cost-effective mining methods.

Underground Mining

Underground mining is carried out when minerals are found deep below the ground. Tunnels and shafts are constructed to reach the mineral deposits.

Placer Mining

Placer mining extracts valuable minerals from riverbeds, beaches, and stream sediments using water to separate heavier minerals.

Solution Mining

Solution mining uses liquids to dissolve underground minerals before pumping the mineral-rich solution back to the surface for processing.
